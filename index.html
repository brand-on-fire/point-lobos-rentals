Welcome to Point Lobos Vacation Rentals

From our beachfront cottages to our ocean view ranch home, we are the vacation rental of choice when visiting Point Lobos, Carmel, Monterey, Pebble Beach, Pacific Grove, Carmel Valley and Big Sur. Enjoy our historic Carmel beachfront cottages with sweeping panoramic ocean views of Point Lobos. Our Acorn Ranch Home is located across from Point Lobos State Reserve with ocean views of Carmel Bay, Pebble Beach and Point Lobos. Surrounded by open space and parkland, the Acorn is a very private setting, yet only a 5-minute drive to Carmel-by-the-sea.
